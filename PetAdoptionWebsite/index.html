Our Story

Rescuing animals,
one home at a time.

Founded in 2013, PawHaven is a no-kill shelter dedicated to finding loving homes for every animal.

Our Mission

Every animal deserves a second chance.

PawHaven operates as a fully no-kill facility. We accept animals from all circumstances and give them the care, rehabilitation, and love they need to thrive.

Our team of 24 full-time staff and over 80 volunteers works 365 days a year.
