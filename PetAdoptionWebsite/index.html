Our Story

Rescuing animals,
one home at a time.

Founded in 2013, PawHaven is a no-kill shelter dedicated to finding loving homes for every animal that comes through our doors — regardless of age, breed, or background.

2,400+
Animals rescued
Our Mission

We believe every animal deserves a second chance.

PawHaven operates as a fully no-kill facility. We accept animals from all circumstances — surrendered, stray, or transferred from overcrowded shelters — and give them the care, rehabilitation, and love they need to thrive.

Our team of 24 full-time staff and over 80 active volunteers works 365 days a year to ensure every animal is healthy, socialised, and ready for their forever family.
